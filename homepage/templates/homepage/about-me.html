About Me

My name is Yasser Jeafar and I'm a Senior at the University of Michigan- Dearborn. My major is Software Engineering and this website was created for my independent study course. I was very interested in building a website using a framework I have never used before, so I went with Django and I'm very glad I did. This website was loosely based off of a database I created for my database course, where I created a very basic UI for it as well. The theme came from my dream to one day own a humble little farm and hopefully be able to retire on it. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to me through the Contact Me page. Thank you for taking the time to look through the website, I really appreciate it!
